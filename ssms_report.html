Inventory Management System


The Somaiya Stationary Management System (SSMS) revolutionizes stationary requisition and distribution in educational institutions, fostering seamless interaction between administrators and faculty. This dynamic platform enables faculty to directly request stationary items, while offering a profile overview of past requests, promoting transparency and accountability. Administrators access a comprehensive array of features, including faculty management, stationary availability tracking, and assignment recording. They efficiently allocate resources, monitor consumption trends, and maintain an inventory of items. The system meticulously documents stationary assignments, highlighting recipients, dates, and quantities. Furthermore, SSMS elevates the decision-making process by granting administrators the capability to generate reports based on tailored parameters. These reports facilitate data-driven insights into assignment patterns, usage trends, and resource allocation, thus fostering informed strategies for improved resource management

SSMS empowers administrators to generate customized reports, facilitating data-driven insights into allocation patterns and resource utilization. This data informs strategic decisions, enhancing overall resource management.

In essence, SSMS merges technology with administrative efficiency, offering an optimized experience for faculty and administrators alike. By improving communication, transparency, and operational agility, SSMS becomes an indispensable tool for efficient stationary management within educational institutions.
