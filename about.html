About Us

Introduction

Established in the academic year 2020-2021, our department offers a comprehensive undergraduate program, B.Tech Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. This program is designed to provide students with knowledge and expertise in the rapidly growing fields of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Science (DS).

AI-DS are interdisciplinary branches of science, engineering, and technology that are revolutionizing various sectors of the technical industry, academia, and research. Our program focuses on equipping students with the necessary skills to create intelligent machines and develop real-world applications using data analytics and visualization technologies.Machine Learning (ML), a subset of AI, enables computers to think and act like humans, while Deep Learning, a subset of ML, allows machines to make decisions and think in a manner similar to humans. By integrating these technologies, our students gain a deep understanding of AI and DS principles, enabling them to tackle complex challenges and drive innovation.

The field of Data Science plays a vital role in today's business landscape. With the exponential growth of data, businesses rely on advanced Big Data Analytics, statistical analysis, data extraction, manipulation, visualization, and data maintenance to identify trends, interpret results, and predict future outcomes.Our curriculum prepares students to be at the forefront of this data-driven revolution, empowering them to excel in the industry.

Vision

  • To serve the society by transforming young minds and preparing them for a bright future.

Mission

  • To create a holistic educational experience for students focused on subject knowledge, problem solving, inquisitiveness, leadership, communication and team skills.
  • To nurture an environment committed to the free exchange of ideas where research, creativity, innovation, and entrepreneurship can flourish.
  • To impact society in a transformative way by getting engaged with the external collaborators.

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

  1. Apply skills required to design and develop solutions applicable in real life situations.
  2. Pursue successful and satisfying careers in the profession of their choice or enterprise.
  3. Pursue higher studies in related fields from recognized institutions.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

  1. Exhibit proficiency in the field of Artificial Intelligence by developing intelligent systems.
  2. Be accomplished in the field of data analytics, statistical programming languages and Machine learning
