Library Policy

  • The library issue time will be between 2.30-4.30 pm every day.
  • Reading room facility will be available 9.00 am to 4.00 pm in room 206.
  • ID Card is required for availing reading room and library facilities.
  • Books will be issued on a first-come-first-served basis.
  • A book will be issued for a duration of 2 weeks at a time.
  • Books will have to be replaced/renewed within two weeks, else a fine of Rs. 25/- will be levied per week.
  • Books may be renewed only if pending requests for the same are not in the queue.
